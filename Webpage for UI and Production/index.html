Project Mosquito Net

.................................................................................................................

Nearly 700 million people get a mosquito-borne illness each year resulting in over one million deaths. Mosquitoes have four distinctive life stages, with the first three stages being spent in the water.

1. Egg Stage
2. Larval Stage
3. Pupal Stage
4. Adult Stage

Mosquitoes breed in stagnant, standing fresh water so our project aims to detect the mosquito breeding grounds. We use drones for the same, and process the video so as to predict the presence of mosquito breeding grounds.
